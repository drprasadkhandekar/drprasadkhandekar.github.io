Academic Profile

Prof. Prasad Khandekar has more than 28 years of Engineering Academics and Industrial experience. He started his career in Automation Industry. He is a Professor in Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Dean Engineering at MIT World Peace University. Additionally, he is also designated as Director Admissions.

Summary

Prof. Prasad Khandekar

Prof. Prasad Khandekar working as Dean, Engineering at MITWPU Pune has a proven experience in setting up academic processes as per latest NBA norms and instrumental role in getting accreditation of two institutes with one of the programs getting 5 years accreditation. He has Strong academic and research interest in microelectronics, CMOS VLSI Design, and Low Power Digital Design. He is a reviewer of international journals; 'The Integration' - VLSI journal of Elsevier and 'DATICS'. He holds strong experience in establishing academic departmental procedures with strong emphasis on research and liaising with government agencies.

About Me

Dr. Prasad Khandekar, is a Professor of Electronics and Communication Engineering having 25 years of teaching experience and 5 years of industry experience. He is a well known name in engineering education for his leadership skills and for his efforts in improving industry readiness of engineering graduates. Presently, he is a Dean of Engineering and Technology, Director Admissions and member of Board of Management at MIT World Peace University, Pune. He has also worked as Director Industry Partnesrships He was a core team member in transformation of MIT institutes on Kothrud Pune campus into Dr Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University, Pune.
He is regularly invited for expressing his views on Indian Higher Education scenario at conferences organized by various Chambers of Commerce, World Education Summit, Print and Electronic media. He was inviited to ABET Symposium 2016 in Florida, USA to present his model of preparing industry ready engineering graduates. He has travelled to France, USA, Austria, Malaysia, Thailand, Sri Lanka, Singapore, Dubai, HongKong and Japan for academic and research partnerships. His statement “Universities have to be ready to compete with third party aggregators who will change the way Universities are functioning now” is being seriously discussed and debated in the higher education circle of India.

Dean Engineering with high ambitions & believe in team work

   Engineering education has seen a sea change in its Teaching- Learning model. It has transformed as ubiquitous now, i.e. anytime, anywhere, anyhow....    
Dr. Prasad Khandekar
Dean, Engineering, Director Admissions & Professor,
School of ECE Engineering.,
MIT World Peace University Pune, INDIA.

Dr. Prasad Khandekar received the B.E. degree in industrial electronics engineering from Pune University, India in 1992 and the M.E. in electronics engineering from Shivaji University, India in 2004. He has been awarded Ph.D. degree in electronics engineering at the Shivaji University, India. From 1992 to 1997, he worked in the field of industrial automation and instrumentation. Ha has worked as an associate professor in electronic and telecommunication department at Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, Pune. He has published papers in 12 international journals, 16 international conferences and three national conferences. He has also two patents published in his credit. His research interests include energy recovery techniques, low power digital design, and CMOS design., Mr. Khandekar is also a life member of Indian Society for Technical Education and life member of Industrial Association of Engineers, Hongkong. resently he Professor in Electronics and Communications Engineering at MIT World Peace University and he is also working as Dean Engineering. He has also been entrusted the profile of Director, admissions at MITWPU Pune INDIA.

The Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) is a multidisciplinary professional engineering institution. The IET was formed in 2006 from two separate institutions: the Institution of Electrical Engineers (IEE), dating back to 1871, and the Institution of Incorporated Engineers (IIE) dating back to 1884.

This is a new professional society I have joined in year 2018. I have attended IET sponsored FDPS. Also participated in professional meets and seminars of IET Pune LN.

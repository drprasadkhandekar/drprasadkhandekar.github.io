Honors and Awards

Prof. Prasad Khandekar has earned credits as an able administrator and as a teacher and also has worked on a number of Honarable positions; including Professional society work, conference sessions, reviewer work, Gust talks on Technology etc. While doing so, he has been honored and awarded as follows...

Honors Credits

Honorary Achievements

  • Invited panel member on CNBC Awaz for discussion on “Pune: The Epicenter of Growth”, September 2017.
  • International Conference on Advanced Computing Technologies and Applications (ICACTA-2015) at DJS COE, Mumbai
  • Invited panel member in 13th FICCI Higher Education Summit 2017 for a panel discussion on “Social Outreach in Higher Education” October 2017
  • A contributing team member in the workshop for enhancing quality of Indian Education during Educon 2014 in Paris, France.

Awards Credits

  • Excellent Performance Award five times since 2007 given by VIIT management.
  • Nominated for the national award, ’Best Low Cost Technology for the Cause of the Disabled’ given away by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment in 2004.
  • “Adarsh Dombivlikar” Award by “Dombivlikar” run by Honorable Ravindra Chavan, Minister of State for IT, Medical Education and Ports. Dombivli is a town in Thane district having 1.4 million populations.
  • “Best Paper Award” in IEEE International Conference ICCUBEA 2015 and 2016.

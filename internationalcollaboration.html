International Visits

Prof. Prasad Khandekar believes in International Networking. He visited several countries for Research Paper Presentations, International Tie-Ups, As a Guest Speaker etc.

UK (January 2020)

Visited UK with the objective of developing "Faculty Familiarisation Program" in collaboration of UK universities. The program is being designed in order to familiarise MIT-WPU faculty with teaching methodologies, administration and other important functions.

Visited ESCP, Eurpe (London Campus), University of Kent, Cardiff University, University of West England, Dudley College, University College of Birmingham, University of Leicester, University oh Hull, Manchester Metropolitan University, University of Manchester and aslo explored the partnership opportunities in developing academic and research collaborations.
